How to Play:

Click Create Game to generate a room code. Then insert your name and click Join Game. If you created the lobby and you are ready to start, click Start. Otherwise, just wait for the host to start. The goal of the game is to find the Afikoman, wrapped in a green napkin. When you find it, click on it and you win!
